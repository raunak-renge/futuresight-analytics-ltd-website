Case Highlights

Financial Services Data Consolidation

The Challenge

A European financial firm was struggling with fragmented reporting systems across multiple business units. Data inconsistencies led to conflicting reports, and manual consolidation consumed weeks of analyst time each quarter.

Our Approach

We designed and built a unified Snowflake-based data warehouse, consolidating data from 12 source systems. We implemented automated ETL pipelines with robust data quality checks and created a suite of Tableau dashboards for executive and operational reporting.

Results
  • 85% reduction in manual reporting effort
  • Single source of truth established across the organisation
  • Quarterly close time reduced from 15 days to 5 days

Global ETL Modernisation

The Challenge

A multinational corporation needed to modernise their legacy on-premises ETL infrastructure. Their existing batch processes were slow, difficult to maintain, and couldn't support real-time reporting requirements.

Our Approach

We migrated critical ETL workflows to Informatica IICS in the cloud, implementing Change Data Capture (CDC) for incremental loads. We optimised data transformation logic and introduced automated monitoring and alerting.

Results
  • 90% reduction in data processing time (from 8 hours to 45 minutes)
  • Real-time data availability for critical business dashboards
  • 40% reduction in cloud infrastructure costs through optimisation

Media & Entertainment AI Innovation

The Challenge

A media company wanted to improve user experience by automatically analysing audio-visual content to generate metadata and recommendations. Their manual tagging process was labour-intensive and inconsistent.

Our Approach

Leveraging our research expertise in multimodal AI, we built machine learning models that analyse both audio and visual features of content. The models automatically classify content, detect key moments, and power personalised recommendations.

Results
  • 95% accuracy in automated content classification
  • 80% reduction in manual tagging effort
  • 30% increase in user engagement through improved recommendations
